| Is_Here of 'a

| Is_Bound of 'a site
| Has_Binding_type of 'a site * binding_type
| Is_Bound_to of 'a site * 'a site

| Bind_to of 'a site * 'a site

| Remove of 'a

| ANY
| FREE
| BOUND
| BOUND_TYPE of binding_type
| BOUND_to of 'a site
